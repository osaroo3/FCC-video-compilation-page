Afro Music

"Afro music" is a broad term encompassing various musical genres and styles that originate from or are heavily influenced by African cultures. It can refer to traditional African music, African popular music (also known as Afropop), or contemporary genres like Afrobeats and Afrohouse.

Afropop

Afropop is a broad term for contemporary African popular music that blends traditional African rhythms, melodies, and musical elements with Western pop styles, often incorporating instruments like guitars, pianos, and electronic production techniques

Afrobeat

Afrobeat is a mesmeric blend of West African and Black American music that has inspired throngs of contemporary acts worldwide with its unique rhythms and melodies.

Afrohouse

Afro House is a subgenre of house music that emerged in South Africa, blending traditional African rhythms and vocals with modern house production techniques.
